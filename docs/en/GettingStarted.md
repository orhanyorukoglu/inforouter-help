# Getting Started

Welcome to infoRouter! This quick guide will help you understand the basics and get productive fast.

![infoRouter Main Portal View](../images/anonymous-intro.jpg)

## Quick Start Checklist

- [ ] Get your infoRouter URL from your administrator
- [ ] Obtain your username and password
- [ ] Access infoRouter through your web browser
- [ ] Log in with your credentials
- [ ] Explore the work area

## First Steps

### Step 1: Access infoRouter

Open your web browser and navigate to your organization's infoRouter URL.

:material-arrow-right: [How to Access infoRouter](AccessingInfoRouter.md)

### Step 2: Log In

Click **"Member Login"** and enter your credentials.

:material-arrow-right: [How to Login](Login.md)

### Step 3: Explore the Interface

Familiarize yourself with the infoRouter work area, including:

- **Libraries** — Your document workspaces
- **Folders** — Document organization containers
- **Documents Pane** — View and manage files
- **Menu System** — Access all features

:material-arrow-right: [The Work Area](WorkArea.md)
