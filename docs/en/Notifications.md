# Notifications

infoRouter supports both on-screen status notifications and SMTP email notifications to keep users informed about document changes and workflow activities.

---

## Notification Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| :material-monitor: **On-Screen** | Status notifications displayed within infoRouter |
| :material-email: **Email** | SMTP email notifications sent to your email address |

---

## When Notifications Are Sent

Email notifications are sent when:

- **Subscribed documents change** — Updates to documents you're subscribed to
- **Subscribed folders change** — New documents or changes in folders you're subscribed to
- **Workflow tasks assigned** — You're assigned a review or approval task
- **Workflow status changes** — Documents you submitted complete workflow steps

---

## Managing Notifications

You can control your notification preferences in your [User Profile](UserProfile.md):

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Notification Type** | None, Instant, or Daily digest |
| **Email Format** | HTML or Plain Text |
| **Attach Documents** | Include documents in notifications |
