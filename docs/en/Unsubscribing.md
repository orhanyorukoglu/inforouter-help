# Subscriptions / Unsubscribing from Documents and Folders

To cancel your subscriptions (unsubscribe) to documents and folders, perform the following:

1. Navigate to your "My Profile" section.
2. Click on the "My Subscriptions" link.
3. The "My Subscriptions" window will appear giving you a full list of your subscriptions.
4. Select the documents and folders to which you wish to cancel your subscription by clicking on the check boxes next to them.
5. Click on the "Unsubscribe" menu item under the "Edit" menu.

Unsubscribing will result in the canceling of all e-mail notifications you receive from infoRouter regarding these documents.
