# Unsubscribing from Documents and Folders

To stop receiving notifications for documents or folders, you need to cancel your subscriptions.

---

## How to Unsubscribe

1. Navigate to your **Personal Dashboard**
2. Click **My Subscriptions**
3. The My Subscriptions window displays all your current subscriptions
4. Check the boxes next to items you want to unsubscribe from
5. Go to **Edit** â†’ **Unsubscribe**

!!! info "Result"
    Unsubscribing cancels all email notifications from infoRouter regarding the selected documents and folders.

---

## Unsubscribing from Individual Items

You can also unsubscribe directly from a document or folder:

1. Navigate to the document or folder
2. Click the **Action** icon
3. Select **Unsubscribe**
