# Selecting columns to view in the documents pane

This window allows you to select the columns you wish to see in the right-hand side of your screen. You can choose to display all or some of the available fields. Your column selections will be remembered by infoRouter.

To select columns for display:

1. Navigate to a folder.
2. Select the "Select Columns" menu item located under the "View" menu.
3. In the window that appears, select from a list of columns on the left and add them by clicking on the "Add" button.

Notice that your settings will remain in effect until you change them.
