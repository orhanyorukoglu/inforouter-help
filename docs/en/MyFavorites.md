# My Favorites

The **My Favorites** view displays documents and folders you've bookmarked for quick access.

---

## Why Use Favorites?

!!! tip "Quick Navigation"
    Favorites help you navigate directly to documents and folders that might be buried deep within the folder structure, saving time and clicks.

---

## Accessing My Favorites

1. Go to your **Personal Dashboard**
2. Click **My Favorites**

---

## Adding to Favorites

To bookmark a document or folder:

1. Navigate to the document or folder
2. Go to **Edit** â†’ **Add to Favorites**

The item is now saved to your Favorites list for quick access.

---

## Managing Favorites

From the My Favorites view, you can:

- Click any item to open it directly
- Remove items you no longer need quick access to
- Organize your frequently accessed content

---

## Use Cases

| Scenario | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| **Frequently accessed documents** | One-click access to daily-use files |
| **Deeply nested folders** | Skip multiple navigation levels |
| **Project folders** | Quick access to current project work |
| **Reference documents** | Easy access to policies and guidelines |
