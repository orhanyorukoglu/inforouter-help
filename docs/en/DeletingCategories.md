# Categories / Deleting Categories

To delete a category perform the following:

1. Click on the "Categories" tab.
2. Click on the small "Delete" icon located next to the Category name in the Categories page.
3. Confirm the delete operation.

Note: The delete icon will only appear if you are a designated "Search and Category Administrator".
